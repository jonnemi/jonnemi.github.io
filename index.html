About

I am a Machine Learning Engineer focusing on vision-language models and human-AI interaction. My research investigates how multimodal models interpret visual concepts and how this compares to human perception by probing the gap between model introspection and model behavior.

Research

VLMs Don't Follow Their Own Introspective Reasoning: When to Call an Apple Red, but Humans Do
Jonathan Nemitz, Michal Golovanevsky, Carsten Eickhoff, Junyi Jessy Li, Kyle Mahowald, William Rudman

We investigate how humans and vision-language models attribute color under controlled visual conditions. Our results reveal systematic differences between human judgments and model predictions, particularly in how models rely on introspective reasoning.
